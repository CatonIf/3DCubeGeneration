Topdown Javascript Redstone

One of the projects I made and I'm proud of is a Nuclear Throne mod, that adds a topdown variant of Minecraft's redstone to the game. I knew it would have been absolutely useless, but it was a fun idea, and interesting to program and optimize. The avaliable pieces are:
  • dust
  • lever
  • torch
  • block
  • piston

The only difference with the NT mod is that torches aren't there, there are just levers switched on.
